What is the Price of Solar Panel for Home in India?

The price of solar panel for home depends on a few key factors:

  • Energy Consumption: The more power you need, the more panels (and higher capacity) your system will require.
  • Type of Solar System: On-grid, off-grid, or hybrid systems each come with different components.
  • Panel Type: Premium options like Monocrystalline are slightly more expensive but offer better efficiency.
  • Brand & Warranty: Trusted brands like Tata Power Solar (used by SuryaMitra) offer longer warranties and durable performance.
